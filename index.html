📱 Install as an App

Piss With Friends can be installed directly on your home screen — no app store required.

iPhone & iPad (Safari only)

1. Open this page in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow at the bottom of the screen)
3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
4. Tap Add — done

Android (Chrome)

1. Open this page in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right
3. Tap Add to Home screen
4. Tap Add — done

The app opens full-screen with no browser chrome, just like a native app.

🔒 New Account System

You can now create an account with a username and password. Your data is tied to your account — not your device — so you can log in from your phone, a friend's phone, or anywhere else and pick up right where you left off.

📖 Personal Diary

Tap My Diary in the nav to see your private stats dashboard. It tracks:

• Lifetime Pees — your all-time total, forever
• Avg Pees / Active Day — how many you average on days you actually pee
• Current & Longest Streak — consecutive days with at least one logged pee
• Leaderboard Wins — days you finished #1
• Avg Leaderboard Placement — your average daily rank
• Most Common Color — your most frequently selected pee color

👤 Public User Bio

Each stat in your diary has a Private / Public toggle. Stats you make public will appear on your user bio for anyone to see. Everything is private by default — you choose what to share.

Tap any player on the leaderboard to view their bio and see the stats they've made public.

🌃 Pee Color Tracker

Before logging a pee, pick the color that matches what you see — from clear all the way to brown. Each color maps to a hydration status so you get instant feedback on how well you're drinking. Your most common color is tracked over time in your diary.

📅 Weekly Leaderboard Reset

The leaderboard resets every Monday. Your weekly pee count starts fresh at zero, giving everyone a clean shot at the top spot each week. Past weeks are gone — only the current week's total counts toward your ranking.

Your personal stats in the diary (lifetime pees, streaks, leaderboard wins) are never reset — those are permanent and kept on your account forever.

🏆 Achievements
